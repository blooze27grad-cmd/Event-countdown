About Bok

A tutor who truly
gets it.

Annyeonghaseyo! Hello! I’m Bok. If you’ve ever felt a bit overwhelmed by the thought of learning Korean, I want you to take a deep breath. You’re in the right place, and I’m so glad you’re here.

My teaching journey started in the heart of Seoul, where I spent seven incredible years helping students connect with the Korean language. Eventually, life brought me to Melbourne, where I continued sharing my love for my native language at a local school. Over the years, I also earned a Master of Education. While I appreciate the academic side of things, my real passion lies in the human side of teaching. I care deeply about making sure you feel heard, supported, and excited to learn.

We won’t be stuck memorizing stiff textbook phrases that nobody actually uses on the streets of Seoul. Instead, we’re going to focus on real, practical conversations and the vibrant culture behind the words. Every single lesson we do together is customized for you. It is a completely relaxed, comfortable space where you can make mistakes, build your confidence, and find your own voice in Korean. Let’s make this journey fun and natural. I can't wait to meet you!
